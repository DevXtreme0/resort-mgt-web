Country:Maldives

The Maldives is a tropical nation in the Indian Ocean composed of 26 ring-shaped atolls, which are made up of more than 1,000 coral islands. It’s known for its beaches, blue lagoons and extensive reefs. The capital, Malé, has a busy fish market, restaurants and shops on the main road, Majeedhee Magu, and 17th-century Hukuru Miskiy (also known as Friday Mosque) made of carved white coral.

Set on a private island in the Maldives' South Ari Atoll, this high-end all-inclusive resort is reached by seaplane from Malé. Upscale suites include living rooms, loft bedrooms and outdoor showers. All feature free Wi-Fi, flat-screens, and minibars, plus tea and coffeemakers. Chic over-water villas add ocean views or kid's bedrooms with game consoles, and upgraded villas offer private pools or terraces with lagoon access. Room service is available.

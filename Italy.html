This modern hotel in a polished building surrounded by mature trees is 4 km from St. Peter's Basilica and 8 km from the Colosseum. The contemporary rooms and suites come with dark wood furniture, free Wi-Fi and flat-screen TVs, as well as tea and coffeemaking facilities. Suites add lounge areas, minibars and cable TV.Breakfast is available for a surcharge. There's also a seasonal outdoor pool, plus sprawling gardens, 2 tennis courts and an exercise room. A spa with an indoor pool welcomes guests age 16 and over.

The Resort has 689 rooms split between typical bungalows (Villaggio) and an elegant hotel. The Villaggio is a labyrinth of narrow streets and pretty paved squares, with flowers galore, just like a real Sicilian village. The bungalows are made of local stone; they have one upper floor and no lift. The hotel, away from the centre of the Resort, has 3 upper floors and a lift. All the rooms have balconies.
